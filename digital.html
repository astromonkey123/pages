KAYZ - Zahn's Airport

Zahn's Airport in North Amityville, NY, was built in 1936 and closed in 1980. It had two runways, and was, in its prime, one of the busiest general aviation airports on the east coast.

KJPX - Town of East Hampton

The Town of East Hampton Airport in East Hampton, NY, serves as the main hub of general aviation traffic into the Hamptons. It sees a large number of helicopter and charter operations, with the occasional Gulfstream. Download Here
