Optimization of Paths

Special Relativity

From Maxwell's equations that govern classical electromagnetism, Einstein concluded that light must travel at the same speed in all reference frames. This assumption destroys much of the physics we have learned up to this point. So, how do we reconcile special relativity with Newtonian Mechanics? How does Special Relativity adjust our understanding of electromagnetism to remove the contradictions created by changing reference frames?
